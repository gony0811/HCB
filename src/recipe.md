### Step Recipe Specification

#### Ingredients
recipe columns 
- `step_number`: The sequential number of the step in the recipe.
- `description`: A detailed description of the step, including any specific instructions or techniques required.
- `duration_time`: The estimated time required to complete the step.
- `force (N)`: The amount of force needed to perform the step, measured in Newtons (N).
